How to Style a Quiff

Styling a quiff starts with blow-drying damp hair upwards using a round brush to create volume. Apply a small amount of pomade or wax to the roots and tips for structure. Comb the hair back and to the side, refining the shape with your fingers. Finish with a strong-hold hairspray to keep the quiff in place all day. Adjust for a sleek, polished look or a more textured, casual style.
